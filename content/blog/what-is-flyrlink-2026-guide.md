## How does AI matching work on Flyrlink?

Most marketplaces give you a keyword search. Flyrlink gives you a ranked shortlist.

Here's what happens when you describe your need:

1. **Parse** - The AI reads your prompt in natural language and extracts intent, domain, complexity, and constraints (budget, timeline, format).
2. **Retrieve** - It scans the full pool of verified experts and filters by skills, availability, pricing, location, and language.
3. **Rank** - A scoring model weighs expertise depth, past review outcomes, response time, session volume, and contextual fit.
4. **Present** - You see a ranked shortlist (typically 3 to 5 experts) with profiles, pricing, and real availability.
5. **Learn** - Every booking and review feeds back into the model so future matches improve.

**The result:** a 2-minute discovery flow that replaces a 2-week one.

**vs LinkedIn.** LinkedIn is a network of profiles. It's not built for discovery, verification, booking, or payment. You cold-DM and hope. Flyrlink packages discovery, verification, scheduling, and payment into one flow.

**vs Traditional consultancies.** A traditional consultant might charge INR 25,000 for a one-hour call you could never schedule. Flyrlink gives you comparable expertise (often the same people, between engagements) at a fraction of the cost, bookable in minutes.

**vs Free Slack/Discord communities.** Communities are great for casual questions. They're slow, unreliable, and unstructured when you need a specific answer in a specific timeframe.
